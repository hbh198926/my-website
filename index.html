Welcome to Hala Bissau

Welcome to Hala Bissau, a welcoming hotel located in Bissau, Guinea-Bissau. Whether you are travelling for business, family vacations, or leisure, our hotel offers comfortable accommodations, friendly service, and convenient facilities to make your stay enjoyable.

Surrounded by beautiful gardens, Hala Bissau features an outdoor swimming pool, a children's playground, free Wi-Fi, and a relaxing atmosphere for guests of all ages. Our air-conditioned rooms are designed for comfort and convenience, with selected rooms offering private balconies overlooking the pool and gardens.

The hotel is conveniently located close to NGO Acção & Desenvolvimento (1.4 km), the American Embassy (2 km), and just 4 km from Osvaldo Vieira International Airport.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your stay at Hala Bissau.

Standard check-in begins at 14:00 and check-out is by 12:00 (noon). Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged subject to availability — please contact our front desk team at +245 95 660 21 60 in advance and we will do our best to accommodate your request.
Yes. Hala Bissau provides a complimentary airport shuttle service to and from Osvaldo Vieira International Airport, which is just 4 km from the hotel. Please advise your flight details at least 24 hours in advance so we can arrange your transfer. Contact us at reservation@halahotelaquapark.com.
Breakfast inclusion depends on your chosen rate plan. Our Bed & Breakfast Rate includes a daily breakfast for two guests at Shahrazad Restaurant (served 07:00–11:00). Other rate plans such as the Flexible Rate are room-only. You can add breakfast during booking or at the hotel. Please check your rate details at reservation.
Hala Bissau has four dining venues open daily:

Shahrazad Restaurant — International all-day dining, open 24 hours.
Dragon Restaurant — Authentic Chinese cuisine, open 24 hours.
Snack Bar — Light meals and snacks, open 15:00–23:00.
Bar Lounge — Cocktails, coffee, and beverages, open 08:00–23:00.

Room service is also available 24 hours a day.
Cancellation policies vary by rate plan selected at time of booking:

Flexible Rate & B&B Rate: Free cancellation up to 48 hours before arrival.
Advance Purchase Rate: Non-refundable once booked.
Long Stay Rates: Free cancellation up to 72 hours before arrival.
Group & Corporate Rates: As per contract terms.

Please review your rate plan details carefully at time of booking. For changes, contact reservation@halahotelaquapark.com.
Yes — complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel including all guest rooms, the lobby, dining venues, pool area, and meeting rooms. Guests can also access our dedicated guest Wi-Fi portal. For group or business events requiring dedicated bandwidth, please contact our events team.
Yes. We have an outdoor swimming pool and Aqua Park open daily from 08:00 to 19:00. The pool is suitable for all ages — children aged 3 and above are welcome. Towels are provided complimentary. Pool attendants are present during all operating hours for your safety and comfort.
Yes. Hala Bissau offers four versatile event spaces:

Nelson Mandela Hall — up to 300 guests.
Amílcar Cabral Hall — up to 100 guests.
Executive Meeting Room — up to 12 guests.
Outdoor Event Space — up to 4,000 guests.

All spaces include professional AV equipment, high-speed Wi-Fi, and dedicated event management support. For enquiries and proposals, contact info@halahotelaquapark.com.
Complimentary on-site parking is available for all hotel guests. The car park is monitored and accessible 24 hours a day. For guests arriving with large vehicles or requiring specific parking arrangements, please contact our front desk in advance.
The hotel accepts payment in XOF (West African CFA Franc), USD, EUR, and other major currencies. We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) as well as cash. Currency exchange services are available at the front desk. Online bookings can be made in USD, EUR, GBP, AED, XOF, BRL.
You can book your stay through multiple channels:

Online: Use the Reserve button on this website to book instantly.
Email: reservation@halahotelaquapark.com
Phone: +245 95 660 21 60
Walk-in: Our front desk is staffed 24 hours a day.

For corporate accounts, group bookings (10+ rooms), or MICE events, please contact our Sales team for preferred rates and availability.
